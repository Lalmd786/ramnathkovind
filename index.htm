    Information about Ram Nath kovind

    Ram Nath Kovind is the ss14th and the present president of India. He assumed the office of president of India on 25th July 2017. He worked extensively in Uttar Pradesh and Bihar. He received his batchelor degree and LLB's degree from DAV college, affiliated to Kanpur University. He was a lawyer who practised in Delhi high court and the supreme court for about sixteen years.
    Ram Nath Kovind was nominated as the presidential candidate by the ruling party NDA coalition.He won the presidential election against Meera Kumar. He began his political carrier in 1994, when he was elected as the member of parliament in the Rajya Sabha from Uttar Pradesh. He was an M.P for two terms from 1996 to 2006.
    He also served as the member of the board of management of Ambedkar University , lauknow and as the member of the board of governor of the Indian Institute of Management, Kolkata. He represented India in the UN in New York and address the UN general assembly in 2002.
    Ram Nath Kovind was borm on 1st october 1945, in Paraukh, near Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. Kovind belongs to the Dalit Koli community. He was born to Maiku Lal and Kalawati. His father was landless poor man who ran asmall shop to support their family. He was the youngest among the three brothers and two sisters. After his elementary school education, he had to walk each day to Kanpur umiversity, which was 8 km away, to attend junior school.
